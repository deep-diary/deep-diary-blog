Introduction of Gallery Project

Blue 2022-08-14 gallery README

说明

​ 本项目主要分为三大维度，第一维度是记忆追溯，是基于深度学习，融合日常视频，语音，图片，文本日记，聊天记录等记忆碎片，使用GCN训练成自己专属的记忆管家，平时您想不起来的事情，她可以帮您想起；第二维度是资源追溯，深入挖掘您的人脉资源，让每个您认识的人作为一个节点，每个节点都包含三大资源及三大需求，同样是基于图卷积网络，让您充分了解您身边朋友的需求及资源。第三维度是去中心化，基于区块链及web3概念，让您的数据更加安全，去中心化，让您的数据产生价值

项目简介

封面--一句话概括项目

==使命：让个人数据产生价值，为自己，也为他人==

==愿景：建立去中心化个人网盘平台，帮助用户低成本存储个人笔记、生活照片、视频等个人数据，让其成为您终身相伴的记忆管家，让您的数据产生价值。==

==价值观：用户至上，诚信==

项目概况

本项目的主要目标为：

让数据强化记忆

==建立去中心化个人网盘平台，帮助用户低成本存储个人笔记、生活照片、视频等个人数据==

让数据产生价值

用户可以决定将个人的数据（隐私数据通过密码学的方式保护起来）贡献出来（通过授权的方式），作为商业数据价值挖掘方的基础数据用于商业价值发现并且产生的收益返回给用户，用户有权收回数据使用方的使用权

将使用数据 训练数据算法比如模型，运行在去中心化计算平台 像bacalhau 平台

让数据创造价值

创造价值指的是资源追溯，深入挖掘您的人脉资源，让每个您认识的人作为一个节点，每个节点都包含三大资源及三大需求，同样是基于图卷积网络，让您充分了解您身边朋友的需求及资源。

第三维度是去中心化，基于区块链及web3概念，通过去中心化技术和模式，实现内容的“可读可写可持有的”，让用户的数据和内容及其产生的价值归属其本身。集成了分布式存储、点对点、非对称加密等技术元素的区块链与Web3.0愿景及理念高度吻合，因而被看作是Web3.0的技术基石

项目亮点

基于深度学习将多维度记忆碎片进行特征融合

基于图卷积神经网络，让您更了解您身边的资源

基于区块链及web3概念，让您的数据更加安全，去中心化，让您的数据产生价值

痛点

  • 日常照片，视频，聊天记录，日记众多，但大部分都被保存到硬盘上，无法进行信息互通，融合，追溯
  • 年龄越来越大，同学会，校友会上会遇到好多优秀的朋友，但单凭人脑记忆，无法记住所有需求及资源，传统数据库记录，也很难匹配相关资源和需求
  • 抖音，朋友圈相关的媒体资源，都有个中心，数据被运营商控制，无法自由控制，处理自己的数据，并未给自己带来一些收益

这些问题如何解决的

深记，是一个旨在解决上述痛点的一个去中心化的平台

如果有这么一个平台，可以使用人脸识别技术，对照片视频出现过的人脸进行自动分类标注，可以使用目标识别技术，对照片中出现的事物进行有效识别并打上标签，可以使用图卷积网络技术，对所有记忆碎片记忆训练，得到真正属于你自己的智能记忆管家

记忆追溯

  • 使用insightface对照片中的人脸进行识别，提取，为照片打上人物信息的标签
  • 使用yolo X 对照片中的目标进行识别并打上事物标签
  • 使用NLP对个人日记，聊天记录进行训练，得到朋友之间的特征关系
  • 使用neo4j图数据库，保存各种维度的阶段，关系
  • 使用GCN对所有的节点特征，节点关系进行训练，得到属于您个人的记忆管家
  • 以django为后台，vue为前台，结合讯飞语音api，对个人记忆进行快速提取、
  • 记忆追溯，旨在于强化您个人的记忆
  • MFT(Multimodality Fusion Technology), combine ，face recognition，object identification

这里的强化记忆，可以理解为记忆追溯，是基于多模态深度学习（Multimodality Fusion Technology，MFT），融合日常视频，语音，图片，文本日记，聊天记录等记忆碎片，使用GCN训练成自己专属的记忆管家，平时您想不起来的事情，她可以帮您想起；

资源追溯

  • 同学会，校友会，忆往昔的同时，我们更倾向于了解别人的需求和资源抱团取暖
  • 聚会期间，使用该平台，录入简单的岗位信息同时，录入您的三大资源，三大需求，三大资源，可以帮您提高额外的收入，三大需求，可以利用平台的的资源，帮您对接解决您的需求。
  • 结合记忆追溯相关技术，再次对相关资源，需求，岗位信息进行融合，实现资源追溯。
  • 资源追溯，旨在于帮您充分挖潜您身边的资源和需求，实现抱团取暖

去中心化

  • 使用区块链技术，让您的数据去中心化，更加安全，只属于您个人的，任何其它机构都无法操纵或者删除您的数据
  • 数据产生价值，深记平台永久免费，一些广告产生的收益，通过区块链技术，让您的数据给您带来收益

Brief Introduction for deep-diary

Cover

==Mission: Make personal data valuable, both for yourself and others==

==Vision: Establish a decentralized personal web disk platform to help users store personal diary, images, videos and other personal data at a low cost, Make all memories searchable and traceable.==

==Values: the user is supreme, prestige first, dedication, honesty a win-win situation==

Problems

  • search: personal data is hard to search and manage
  • centralized: many current web albums are centralized and the data only belongs to operators
  • dimension: It's hard to share social resources

Solutions

deep-diary, which is based on deep leaning and web3 technology, extract features from your pictures, videos, diary text, chatting records etc., then fuse and train all the features together. in the other words, it could fuse all the shard of your memories, which could help you remember your friends' detail information, resources and needed, help you remember all your experience, like travel, parties, activities, help you find the relationship among friends, resources and needs. for the storage, all the resource will be stored in the decentralized storage server and will be more safe and are only belongs to you, which could bring you some incoming if any body visit your resources.

Image Process

after uploading the img, our system will do those process:

  • synchronize the resource to the MCS
  • auto tagging
  • auto category
  • face recognition
  • fetch colors
  • fetch any other exif(Exchangeable Image File) info like GPS , date, layout(wide or tall), etc.

mcs storage

for the storage, Unlike traditional web2.0 network disk, which have the features of centralized, so it will be hard controlled by the user, for this project, we are using the MCS(Multi-Chain Storage) as the default storage. which will be decentralized and more safe for the data

auto category

for the auto category, it is based on the deep learning. and the accuracy will be improved by iterative loop like this.

  • predefine the categories and prepare some images
  • training the model
  • do the auto category, then do the Manual correction for our own image.
  • if someone could share the high accuracy category images, we could do the training again, and the result will be more better

in the near future, we will provide some service that the user could train their own data

face recognition

face recognition could help you auto tagging the img with the personal name, which will be helpful to organize or search your images based on the face names or face numbers

fetch colors

what dose fetch colors means:

let's make an example

pls watch the this image, which is a beautiful girl. we could fetch colors based on AI(artificial intelligent), or called deep learning. this image mainly have those parts.

  • background: white, ice yellow, curtain, skin
  • foreground: brown hair, light brown, skin
  • total image: white, skin, brown

why shall we need to fetch colors

If we uploads tons of product images to the system, we would have to identify and assign the right colors for each product. This can be hard for us and also requires time that could otherwise be spent on doing something more creative and productive. Our color extraction solves this automatically.

based on which, we could do the color search both based on the background color, foreground color, or the total image colors. it will be interest, isn't it.

after fetching the color, we could search the image based on the color attribute

fetch any other exif information

other exif info are like GPS , date, layout(wide or tall), etc. which have 2 advantages:

  • make our image searchable by location or city name, date and layout
  • show all the images through map dimension based on the GPS

Event Diary

the concept of deep-diary is based on event, not the daily, the event could be very small, even like: take a photo.

and also, we call this event as a fragment of memory.

after we recording the events in the daily life, we could piece together the fragment of memory through GCN to create your own memories.
